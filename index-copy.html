Hello, my name is Matthew Herren.

I'm a user experience and user interface designer with experience in a wide variety of technologies and target audiences. In all of my work I focus on creating highly intuitive, straightforward and beautiful experiences on everything from static web sites to voice controlled smart home platforms.

It's nice to meet you...

I'm a freelance designer I take on projects for clients around the world, working either remotely or on site. I have a broad background that stretches from technology startup world all the way to the nuts and bolts of building large complex applications on the likes Amazon Web Services. 

However, regardless of what is behind the scenes it is the design of interface that users will interact with. And that is where I find challenge, excitement and reward.
